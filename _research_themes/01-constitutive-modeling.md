We develop and calibrate advanced constitutive models for granular soils and rock, capturing
key mechanical responses including nonlinearity, anisotropy, and cyclic behavior. Our work
integrates laboratory element testing with numerical model implementation to support accurate
finite-element and finite-difference analyses.
