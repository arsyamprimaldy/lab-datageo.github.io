We advance methods for subsurface characterization using in-situ testing (CPT, SPT, DMT, PMT)
and geophysical surveys. Our research focuses on data quality assessment, interpretation
frameworks, and machine-learning-assisted soil profiling to improve the reliability of site
investigation programs.
