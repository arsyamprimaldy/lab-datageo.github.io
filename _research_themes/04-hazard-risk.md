We develop probabilistic frameworks for natural hazard characterization—including landslides,
liquefaction, and ground subsidence—at the regional scale. Our approaches integrate remote
sensing, GIS, and reliability methods to support land-use planning, early-warning systems,
and infrastructure resilience evaluation.
